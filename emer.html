Emergency Services
In case of emergency, call 911 or your local emergency services immediately.

Medical Emergency

Call 102 for ambulance services or visit the nearest hospital for urgent medical care.

Fire Emergency

Call 101 to report a fire. Evacuate the area and follow safety protocols.

Police Assistance

Call 100 if you need immediate police support in case of danger or crime.

Disaster Relief

Call 108 for disaster management and rescue operations during natural calamities.

Poison Control

Call the poison control helpline if someone has ingested toxic substances.

Women & Child Helpline

Call 1091 for women's safety or 1098 for child helpline services.
